Statistical Methods

We are interested in providing statistical solutions to biology-driven questions. We have expertise in the design of experiments and develop high-performance statistical methods to process high-throughput data, including those obtained through microarrays and next-generation sequencing.

We are the authors of crlmm, a fast algorithm for preprocessing and genotyping Affymetrix and Illumina SNP arrays.

Software

We make constant use of open-source and free software to develop and deploy our solutions. Our software solutions are currently distributed via Bioconductor, serving thousands of users worldwide.

Our oligo package gives support to several dozens of microarray designs produced by Affymetrix and Nimblegen. It offers preprocessing tools for Expression, Gene ST, Exon ST, HTA and SNP arrays.

The crlmm package provides optmized support for Affymetrix and Illumina SNP arrays for genotyping and copy number analyses.

The Rqc package offers the user efficient strategies for quality assessment of Next-Generation Sequencing data. It processes FASTQ and BAM files (whole files or samples of thereof) without compromising the system performance, allowing the user to evaluate possible issues during the sequencing experiment.

Training

We deliver training in analysis of biological data, including RNA-Seq and Whole Exome Sequencing data with international partners like the EBI/UK.

We provide training in Brazil and abroad on the following main topics:

  • Differential Expression - Microarrays
  • Differential Expression - RNA-Seq
  • Copy Number and Genotyping - Microarrays
  • Variant Calling - Exome Sequencing

We focus on the use of open-source and free software, in particular solutions provided via the Bioconductor project.
